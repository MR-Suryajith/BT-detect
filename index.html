Curious Minds Want to Know

What are the common symptoms of brain tumours?

Headaches, seizures, memory problems, and vision changes are common symptoms.

How can I reduce my risk of developing a brain tumour?

Maintain a healthy lifestyle, avoid radiation exposure, and protect your head from injuries.

Are all brain tumours cancerous?

No, some brain tumours are non-cancerous and can be successfully treated.

What treatments are available for brain tumours?

Treatment options include surgery, radiation therapy, and chemotherapy.

Can brain tumours be prevented?

While not always preventable, early detection and healthy habits can reduce the risk.
